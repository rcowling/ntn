About this Application

This application is meant to provide a source of information for our current and future trail riders. Within the application you can find the location of our trailheads and trails. Information is provided about each trail such as the trails difficulty, length, elevation and usage type. This application was developed by Bob Cowling for a graduate project at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. For more information please contact rcowling @ wisc.edu.
