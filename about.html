About our Trails
Our trail system included in this application has the following trails in Marquette County:
  • Forestville (nordic skiing, showshoeing)
  • Big Pay Pathway (nordic skiing)
  • Saux Head (nordic skiing)
  • Fit Strip (nordic skiing/snowshoeing)
  • Marquette North Trails (snowshoeing, snowbiking)
  • Marquette South Trails (snowshoeing, snowbiking)

    This application is meant to provide a source of information for our current and future trail riders. Within the application you can find the location of our trailheads and trails. Information is provided about each trail such as the trails difficulty, length, elevation and usage type.
