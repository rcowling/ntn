Become a Member
Options: We have a selection of memberships to suit your interests. Review the categories below to find the membership right for you and your family. If you have questions, please call us at 906-235-6861 or stop in the NTN office at Lakeview Arena in Marquette.

Membership Term: If you join or renew between March 1 and August 31, your membership carries you through until April 1 of the following year. If you join/renew between September 1 and February 28, your membership carries you through October 1 of the following year. Watch your snail mail for a renewal reminder postcard!

Day passes: Available below or you can contribute at any of our trailheads. Suggested $10/day donation.
